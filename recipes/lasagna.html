Lasagna

(Image source: Wikimedia Commons)

Description

Lasagna noodles are a staple in Italian cuisine, perfect for making traditional lasagna dishes. These wide, flat pasta sheets can be used for layering with cheese, sauce, and meat or vegetables to create a delicious meal. Making your own noodles allows you to control the texture and flavor, giving you a homemade touch to your lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and salt in a bowl, then create a well in the center.
  2. Crack the eggs into the well and add olive oil.
  3. Gradually mix the eggs into the flour and knead the dough until smooth, about 10 minutes.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Roll out the dough into thin sheets and cut them into lasagna noodles.
  6. Boil the noodles in salted water for about 2-3 minutes, then drain.
  7. Use the noodles for your lasagna recipe or store them for later use.
