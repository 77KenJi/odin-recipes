Tacos

(Image source: Wikimedia Commons)

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish made from a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as seasoned meat, beans, cheese, salsa, and other toppings. They’re easy to prepare and make for a fun meal. Tacos are customizable, so you can enjoy them in many different ways.

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • ½ cup shredded cheese
  • ½ cup salsa
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lettuce, shredded

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a pan, breaking it up as it cooks.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water, then simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  4. Fill each tortilla with the cooked meat, cheese, salsa, lettuce, and cilantro.
  5. Serve with your favorite taco toppings and enjoy!
